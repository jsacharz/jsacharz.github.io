What is PROTEOMICS?


Proteomics is a part of omnics studies which analysis the entire protein content in a sample. Proteins are the very basic bricks building your body but more importantly they play roles of enzymes, receptors, and other do-ers in a biological laboratory within your body. You need these reactions in order for your body to live by preforming functions to name a few: metabolism, learning but also fight infections... Which takes us right to COVID.

Can PROTEOMICS predict your COVID odds?

Every-body is different. There is no standard Proteome-fingerprint for an individual person. Analysis of proteomes of large number of COVID patients prior and during infections allows us to assign standards. Information gathered on the regulatory trends of proteins allows us to divide COVID cases into severity catergories and assign the proteomic fingerprint for patenits who have no symptoms, show them mildly only or suffer severily, and in extreme cases die. This combined with mcahine learning turns could help to predict the potential outcomes of infection and help with the personalization of treatment.
